Tengyue LI

Greetings! My name is Tengyue LI (Chinese: 李腾跃, Email: litengyue@jlu.edu.cn). I am currently a postdoctoral researcher under the guidance of Prof. Wen Zhang at the Rock Mass Structure and Geological Hazards Lab (RMSGHL), which is part of the College of Construction Engineering at the Jilin University of China.

I received my Bachelor, Master, and PhD degrees from Ocean University of China in 2013, 2015, and 2022, respectively. I was funded by the China Scholarship Council (CSC) to carry out my research in image analysis at University of Leicester of United Kindom during my PhD period. I ever worked at Hisense as a hardware engineering designer.
